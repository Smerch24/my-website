Complete transformation of the kitchen on the 4th floor
Complete transformation of the kitchen on the 4th floor of the student dormitory! We have carried out renovations: we have installed modern appliances, as well as made a new floor and ceiling, installed new energy-saving windows, completely replaced the plumbing and pipes, and carried out cosmetic repairs to the walls. Now cooking your favorite dishes has become even more pleasant and convenient.
«Ukrainian National h-index Ranking 2024»
According to the NMBS Scopus rating "Ukrainian National h-index Ranking 2024", the Business College entered the 100 best scientific institutions of Ukraine, occupying the 84th position. In addition to the consolidated rating "Ukrainian National h-index Ranking", which evaluates scientific productivity within the country based on Hirsch index indicators, scientific institutions are also represented in the section of scientometric databases Scopus and Web of Science. Cherkasy State Business College took the 84th position (h-index – 4) according to the NMBD Scopus indicator. We congratulate the scientists of ChDBK and wish them further scientific achievements!
Сreative meeting with the writer
On April 18, students of groups #1P-22, 2P-22 held a creative meeting with writers Kateryna Verbivska and Tatiana Brooks. Since modern Ukrainian literature completes the study of the school course, Mrs. Kateryna outlined features of the literary process from 1991 to the present day, recited her own new works, and Ms. Tetiana interested the youth prose, written under the influence of a twelve-year stay in the USA (the state of Alaska), told about the peculiarities translation verlibres of the Cherkasy professor, doctor of philological sciences Vasyl Pakharenko in English, and students shared impressions from the works of modern novelists they read. The meeting ended with the wishes of the guests to the students for further growth and realization of their own bravest ideas and dreams but at the same time do not forget that reading? it is the way of development of a person, his imagination and creativity, enrichment own spiritual world.
